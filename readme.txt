== Description ==

WP Ever Accounting is the most advanced accounting plugin for WordPress. Keep track of your income and expenses, and send professional invoices in multiple currencies. WP Ever Accounting provides everything you need to boldly run your business.

It provides small businesses with a one-stop accounting solution. The plugin's primary goal is to provide a simple yet professional way for a business to manage its finances, keep track of income and expenses, and provide details and powerful reports to help it understand its financial position.

You can use the plugin to manage your income and expenses without any prior accounting knowledge. It will assist you in overcoming accounting-related issues and focusing more on other business operations.

Managing a small business has many challenges, and using the best solution can help you avoid some of them. One of the most important tasks for any business is to keep track of its cash flow.
You will stay on top of your accounting tasks if you can create income and expenses in multiple currencies. Aside from that, opening multiple accounts and transferring funds between them will make accounting operations easier.

* **MULTI-CURRENCY:** Multi-currency accounting allows you to conduct business all over the world. Track your income and expenses in any currency you want, and the plugin will convert them to your main currency.

* **CASH FLOW:** Get a real-time overview of your cash flow. Stay on top of your cash flow and make better business decisions at any time. Real-time visibility into your income, expenses, and revenues.

* **EASY INVOICING:** Create professional invoices in your customer's currency. Invoices can be easily printed and shared. The invoice also allows for tax calculation and discounts.

* **BILLABLE EXPENSES:** Create and manage bills to ensure that your finances are always accurate and in good shape. Print the bill and give it to your vendor. Bill also allows for tax calculation and discounts.

* **EXPENSE TRACKING:** Create and manage your business expenses in any currency you prefer, ensuring that your finances are always accurate and in good shape. Understand what and when to pay.

* **INCOME TRACKING:** Create and manage your business income in any currency you want, ensuring that your finances are always accurate and in good shape. Understand what you will be paid for and when you will be paid.

* **ITEMS AS PRODUCT:** Items can be used as products or services. When creating invoices and bills, you can use items to include price, tax, and other fields.

* **TRANSACTION CATEGORIES:** Create income and expense categories to see how your business is performing at a glance. Keep track of which categories in your company are spending the most and making the most money.

* **POWERFUL REPORTING:** Get detailed financial reports to help you visualize all of the information you need to improve your business. Create smart financial reports and budgets to help you understand and track what matters most.

* **CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT:** Create customers to assign revenues to, and then filter the transactions you've done with them. You can save a customer's name, address, email, phone number, and so on.

* **VENDOR MANAGEMENT:** Create vendors to assign payments to, and then filter the transactions you've done with them. You can save a vendor's name, address, email, phone number, and so on.

== Installation ==

= Minimum requirements =

* WordPress 4.9 or greater
* PHP version 7.4 or greater
* MySQL version 5.6 or greater
* MariaDB version 10 or later

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option as WordPress handles the file transfers itself and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of WP Ever Accounting, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click "Add New".

In the search field type "WP Ever Accounting" and click Search Plugins. Once you have found the plugin you can view details about it such as plugin version, rating, and description. Most importantly, of course, you can install it by simply clicking "Install Now".

= Manual installation =

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/wp-ever-accounting` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work like a charm; as always though, ensure you backup your site just in case.

Apart from a fully functional WordPress installation, WP Ever Accounting does not have any dependency. The minimum required version of WordPress is 4.7.0.
